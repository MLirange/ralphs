For booking information, contact ralphsbooking@gmail.com.

General Booking Info

  • Contact us if interested in booking a full night.
    • There is rarely room to add individual acts, so do not contact us asking to "hop on a bill".
  • Music generally starts at 9pm with an 8pm door time.
  • Music has a hard 1am cutoff time.
  • Cover charge is determined by the promoter booking the evening.
  • We ask that you please do your part on the promotional front with a flyer (online and printed at the club) and at least a Facebook event page. More is encouraged and will only increase your chances of getting booked again.

Venue Info

  • Stage Dimensions: 20′ (width) x 7.5′ (height) x 15′ (depth)
  • I/O:
    • Behringer X32 Producer
    • Behringer S32 Digital Stagebox
    • 32 XLR Inputs
    • 16 XLR Outputs (7 available to bands)
  • Monitors:
    • 2 Separate Front-Of-Stage Monitor Mixes. 2x15 each.
    • 1 Individual 1x15 Drum Monitor
  • More technical information available upon request.
